CSS Specificity Order

The short answer:

  1. Inline Styles (NEVER USE THEM)
  2. ID's: #home, #nav-bar
  3. Classes, attributes & pseudo-classes: .home, [class], :hover

The long answer:

  • The more specific you select something, the higher the specificity. (i.e. "nav a {}" is more specific then "a {}")
