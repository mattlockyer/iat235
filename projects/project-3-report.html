Project 3: Report

Description

Based on your final project you will be required to provide well researched design rationale for your final IA and User Experience.

The purpose of this report is to articulate to your clients (TAs and myself) your design decisions, using the design, information architecture, user experience and technical material covered in this course.

If you cannot defend your design ideas, you will lose marks.

If you don't have a good reason for your designs, be honest, do some research and tell us why they don't work.

Format

  1. Introduction - team, section, general concept
  2. Features - summarize key changes, don't explain how they work
  3. Key Changes (at least 4)

    1. What is the difference from the original site (Craigslist)
    2. Images of the change
    3. Where did the idea come from? (optional: include an image)
    4. What are some design aspects of this change? Is it structural, surface, etc...?
    5. What are your perceived benefits for the user? Is it better? Why?
    6. PROVIDE REFERENCES AND EXAMPLES

  4. Summary - what works about the redesign as a whole. Talk about the changes, bring it all together.

Submission Instructions

These reports are to be completed individually and in your own words!

Plagiarism will result in 0 marks.
