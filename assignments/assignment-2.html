Assignment 2: Wireframing

Description

Working with the wireframe sketches from your deliverables for P02 you will be developing interactive wireframes to begin testing our ability to move through the content.

Due

Before your Week 5 lab.

Worth

5% of your final grade

Instructions

Please note the following wireframing rules:

  • use a clear grid structure
  • no branded elements are to be included (placeholders only)
  • only shades of grey, one orange, one blue, black and white are permitted for colours
  • only different weights of Arial is permitted
  • different pages should link to one-another appropriately

Steps to completing the assignment:

  1. Create 3-5 pages of wireframes in Draw.io / Axure demonstrating the screens a user might encounter while posting an item in your section on Craigslist:

    • You are including real text in your wireframes (no Lorem Ipsum)
    • Your wireframes must allow us to navigate between steps (using IMG MAPS)

  2. Create notes on each of the wireframes that help illustrate how you are fulfilling functional and content specifications:

    • The notes should identify specific specifications, and how the wireframes are demonstrating fulfilling them
    • Ensure the notes are embedded on the appropriate pages within the wireframes

  3. Put all HTML files into a folder for your submission, and ZIP the FOLDER into a single zip file.

  4. Put this on your SIAT website and bring the link to your wireframes to lab for critique.

Final Delivery

Final deliverables are due to Canvas before your lab time on Week 5. Please remember that professionalism in your submission is important:

  • 1 HTML IMG MAP wireframe prototype (HTML in ZIP)
  • 1 LINK to the LIVE copy of your wireframes for lab

Grading Rubric

Each requirement receives points as outlined:

  • There are 3-5 interactive wireframes (1 pt)
  • Wireframes follow the required rules (1 pt)
  • The wireframes show clear consideration of hierarchy and structure (1 pt)
  • Notes cite specific criteria that the wireframes effectively fulfill (2 pts)
